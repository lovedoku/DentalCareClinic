Principal Surgeon

Meet Our Principal Dental Surgeon, Dr. Samuel Tetteh Djaba We are proud to introduce Dr. Samuel Tetteh Djaba, our esteemed Principal Dental Surgeon, to the Dental Care Clinic family. Dr. Djaba is a distinguished Fellow of the Ghana College of Surgeons and the Ghana Dental Association, with a wealth of experience and expertise in the field of dentistry. Specializations and Credentials Dr. Djaba specializes in Forensic and Restorative Dentistry, and his dedication to excellence is evident in every aspect of his work. With a passion for education and a commitment to staying at the forefront of dental advancements, Dr. Djaba has served as a lecturer at the University of Ghana Dental School, Korle-bu, Accra, where he imparted his knowledge and expertise to future generations of dental professionals. In addition to his impressive credentials in Ghana, Dr. Djaba is also a graduate of the University of London (King's College), where he obtained a Master of Clinical Dentistry in Prosthodontics. This international experience has further enriched Dr. Djaba's skills and knowledge, allowing him to provide the highest level of care to his patients.Commitment to Education and Excellence Dr. Djaba's commitment to education and excellence in dental care is unwavering. He believes in empowering his patients with the knowledge and tools they need to maintain optimal oral health and achieve beautiful smiles that last a lifetime. Dr. Djaba takes the time to listen to his patients' concerns, understand their goals, and tailor personalized treatment plans to meet their individual needs. As the Principal Dental Surgeon at Dental Care Clinic, Dr. Djaba leads by example, demonstrating compassion, integrity, and professionalism in everything he does. He is deeply committed to providing each patient with the highest standard of care in a warm and welcoming environment, ensuring their comfort and well-being throughout their dental journey.
